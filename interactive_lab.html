Each card shows the guard's learned probability of watching each room. We save one card while training goes on. Brighter = watched more often.

Snapshots will appear here as the experiment runs.
Method: REINFORCE with a baseline (Monte-Carlo policy gradient). The policy is softmax-with-temperature and the thief uses a prudent shortest-path.
